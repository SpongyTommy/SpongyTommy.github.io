My Friends...

I have many friends in my life, however my number one would have to be Marcus Scullion, I have known him for about nine years now however I have not seen him for two years now. My second best friend would have to be Thomas Groves he used to be in my pre school with Marcus but I have not seen him for five years. Nevertheless my third best friend would have to be Findlay Harper, we used to be really good freinds in Crawley and we sometimes have the odd Skype every so often.

My Family...

I am in a family of four, I have a very cute sister, a very sporty dad, and a loving mum. I will start of with my little sister, she is a Batman fan who loves football. My sporty dad really tries to push me, he is that fit he did the hundred km hike in Hong Kong, my mum is a phenominal cooker nevertheless she is always up for a laugh.

Hong Kong...

In Hong Kong it is very hot and humid, I moved to Hong Kong becuase of my dads buisness HSBC. Like I said Hong Kong is a great place to live because you can make so many friends and the theme parks such as Ocean Park and Disney Land is all pheneminal. Hong Kong has great hikes over in Sai Kung which I do with my Scouts group. Adding on to that I do many Scout Camps over all over the place in Hong Kong with my mum.

Kellett School

I am in an International School in Hong Kong known as Kellett, they do not know where they got the name from. The story starts here, there wasn't enough room for kids to go to school in Hong Kong, so people parents got fed up of it so they formed a group called Parents Together. And they designed a starter school in Wan Chai where around 200 students fit in there. Then they built a campus in Pok Fu Lam where 450 students fit, and here I am now in the new Kowloon Bay Campus.
